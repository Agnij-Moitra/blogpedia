Dear Diary,
We, humanity, are struck by a strange enemy called the Novel Corona Virus aka COVID-19, and the world is brought to its knees by this small virus. It originated in Wuhan city of the Hubei Province of China and since then it has spread like a wildfire through the length and breadth of the world. To combat this menace many countries have gone into lockdown so that the Virus can be contained.

Lockdown means to stop all activities except the essential services to promote social distancing. According to medical advice, the virus spreads through droplets which can generally travel from one to two meters, and therefore social distancing is the only way to break the chain of transmission. If we maintain social distancing and avoid going to markets, then the probability of getting infected reduces. In our country nationwide lockdown was imposed on March 24th, 2020. This meant everyone was expected to stay where ever they were. Offices, Schools, Colleges, Restaurants were shut down for 21 days. All modes of domestic and international transport were suspended except the movement of essential goods.
